What is Fourth Quest?

Our name, Fourth Quest, is inspired by the Vedic Sanskrit word "Tureeya," meaning "the fourth and the ultimate state of consciousness." Fourth Quest embodies the journey beyond basic needs to a quest for knowledge and improvement. We aim to be your partner in research and learning, driving innovation and excellence.
